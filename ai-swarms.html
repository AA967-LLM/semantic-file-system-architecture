Running a 100-agent swarm entirely on premium cloud endpoints is a math error. Here is the local-cloud hybrid architecture that cuts API burn by 92% without dropping IQ.

The Math

Ollama + DeepSeek = Infinite Scale

Instead of paying premium tokens for simple data extraction or logic formatting, the AI Council routes sub-tasks dynamically. Heavy lifting goes to Delta (DeepSeek V4 Pro). Pure math and text goes to Zeta (Ollama / VibeThinker).

Hybrid Cost Formula
$$ \text{Total Cost} = \sum (\text{Task}_{\text{complex}} \cdot C_{\text{API}}) + \sum (\text{Task}_{\text{simple}} \cdot 0) $$
Plain English: By running the simple tasks on your own computer for free ($0), you only pay for the complex stuff. This drops your total API bill drastically, allowing you to spawn hundreds of agents at the cost of ten.
